Travel Q&A

What airport should I fly into?

San Diego Airport (SAN)!


Is parking available at the venue?

Fruitcraft does not have on-site parking. We highly recommend using rideshare services (Uber/Lyft). For guests who prefer to drive, here are some parking options:

The DMV Lot (3960 Normal St): Located just one block away. The lot is free and available to the public after 5:00 PM on weekdays and on weekends.

Street Parking: Metered street parking is available along University Ave and nearby Herbert St, but spots can be limited.


I'll be there a bit early and am looking for something fun to do. Any recommendations?

You are in luck, the venue is close to some great spots; including the San Diego Zoo, Balboa Park, Panama 66 (where Diana & Michael had their first date), Footnote Bookstore, and Sunnyboy Biscuit Co.!
